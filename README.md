# Store Sales Insights – Power BI Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Store Sales Insights Dashboard** built using Power BI.  
The dashboard analyzes retail sales data to track revenue, profit, quantity sold, customer contribution, category performance, payment modes, and regional sales trends.  
It is designed to help stakeholders quickly understand business performance and identify growth opportunities.

## Business Objective
The goal of this project is to:
- Monitor overall store performance using key KPIs
- Identify top customers, products, and categories
- Analyze profit trends over time
- Understand regional sales distribution
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior and payment preferences


## Data Sources
- **Orders.csv** – Transaction-level order data  
- **Details.csv** – Product, customer, category, and payment details  

Data is used for academic and portfolio purposes only.

##  Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI Desktop**
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
- **DAX** – Calculated measures and KPIs
- **CSV / Excel datasets**

##  Dashboard Components

### Sales Overview
- KPI cards for Sales, Profit, Quantity, and AOV
- Interactive slicers for **Quarter (Qtr 1–Qtr 4)** and **State**

### Customer Analysis
- **Sum of Amount by Customer Name**
- Identifies top revenue-generating customers such as *Harivansh* and *Priyanka*

### Category Performance
- **Sum of Quantity by Category**
  - Clothing: ~64.7%
  - Electronics: ~21.1%
  - Furniture: ~14.2%

### Profit Trend Analysis
- **Profit by Month**
- Visual comparison across October, November, and December
- Highlights decline in profit during December

### Regional Analysis
- **Sum of Amount by State**
- Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh as top contributing states

### Payment Mode Analysis
- **Sum of Quantity by Payment Mode**
  - COD (~46%) as the most used payment method
  - EMI, Debit Card, and other digital methods follow

### Product Sub-Category Profit
- **Sum of Profit by Sub-Category**
- Saree and Bookcases contribute the highest profit among sub-categories

## Key Insights
- Clothing dominates sales quantity, indicating strong customer demand.
- COD remains the preferred payment method, suggesting trust or accessibility factors.
- A noticeable profit drop in December signals potential seasonal or operational issues.
- Sales are concentrated in a few key states, indicating regional dependency.
- A small number of customers contribute a large share of total revenue.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus promotional strategies on high-performing categories like Clothing.
- Investigate reasons for profit decline in December to improve seasonal planning.
- Encourage digital payment methods to reduce COD dependency.
- Expand marketing and logistics efforts in high-performing states.
- Introduce loyalty programs for top customers to retain high-value clients.
